Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Please browse through the answers to common questions we get.

How do I delete my account?

The easiest way is to email us. Click for an expansion on details.

Currently, there is no method for a user to manually delete their account themself. We are still in the early stages of building up our database and interactivity. With the small number of users currently, our team is capable of manually handling deletion requests. Please email us through our email link at the bottom of the page or project.hangout@yahoo.com. In the email please include your username, email used for account creation if different, and screenshot of account logged in as proof of account ownership. We use this as a means of ensuring malicious individuals cannot impersonate others to delete accounts, and we will never ask for your password. Rolling out user ability for account deletion is a project we hope to finish someday, but emailing us is the best method for now.

How do I join an event?

Check description for contact details, and events are often public entry unless stated otherwise. Click for an expansion on details.

When creating an event, the poster gives information on the location and time of the event. In the description, it is expected they will include any preferred contact method and potential restrictions for entry. If the description is missing contact information, try finding the event through its name on the MGA website or email.

How do I create an event?

Use the Create page found on the top left. Click for an expansion on details.

In the top left of the screen, a navbar contains clickable links to the other pages. Second on the list and left of FAQ is Create. Clicking Create will take you to the Create page. You can also use the Create link above in the clickable dropdown. Event name, description, location, event date, event time, and campus should be done for the submission of an event. The event will not immediately appear in the Events page. We will check any created events for potential concerns before letting other users see them. We check for new event regularly, and it should not take long for your event to be approved or denied. Contact information for reaching you or event organizers in the description is recommended for other users looking to reach out about your posted event.

What is the amount of time it takes for my event to post?

The average time would likely be 24 hours or less. Click for an expansion on details.

We check regularly for any new events being made. While not instantenous, we take the safety of our community seriously. By checking events before allowing people to see them, users are less likely to encounter fake or malicious events. If your event has taken longer than 24 hours for approval or you feel had rejection incorrectly, please email or call us to discuss and review the issue. The Hangout Board is a safe and friendly environment, and our team thanks you for your patience and understanding.

Can I change my password?

Yes, use the forgot password in the login screen. Click for an expansion on details.

When logging in, there is a forgot password button that brings you to the forgot password page. It will ask for your username or email. Once you send us the request, we will contact you with a way to change your password. While it is for those who forgot their password, it can also be used to change it. On the dropdown, forgot password should work as a clickable link to the page.
