The Hangout Board

Our Purpose

The purpose of The Hangout Board is to connect students across the Middle Georgia State University campuses. Members of MGA can submit their events to The Hangout Board. A review from an admin will decide if the event becomes public. Once public, people can view the information on the event. Event name, description, and location are key information for these posts. A login and create account feature are available. With a focus on creating and viewing events, The Hangout Board aims to become the primary method the MGA community finds and joins the various events found around the campuses. Our ambitions began with Zack Weldon at the start of the Spring 2026 semester. While brainstorming ideas for a Capstone project, The Hangout Board came to be. After presenting his idea, the growing team for the Capstone project refined the idea further. Zack, John, Jason, Drew, and Deegan comprise the team. Each member brought a series of talents and fresh ideas. February saw the creation of a rough draft for the website. Work on a database began late in February. Cloudflare resources and Github storage became crucial parts of the The Hangout Board's efficient creation.
